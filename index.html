Why Chess

Benefits of Learning Chess

Enhanced Focus

Every game builds the kind of laser focus that follows them into classrooms, conversations, and everything that actually matters..

Better Memory

Chess doesn't just exercise the brain — it rewires it. Players instinctively start storing patterns, recalling sequences, and thinking ahead..

Strategic Thinking

Most people react. Chess players anticipate. Your child learns to think 3 moves ahead — not just on the board, but in arguments, decisions, and every room they walk into .

Sportsmanship

Losing is the most valuable lesson chess teaches — and it teaches it without lectures. Children learn to sit across from defeat, shake hands, and come back stronger. .

Academic Growth

The logic, patience, and pattern recognition built at the board has a documented habit of showing up on report cards.

Social Skills

There's a quiet confidence that comes from competing face-to-face, reading opponents, and holding your nerve under pressure. Chess builds that. Your child doesn't just learn a game — they learn how to hold their own in the world..

About Our Academy

Our Background

Sri Sai Chess Academy is dedicated to nurturing young minds through structured and skill-based training. With over 5+ years of coaching experience, the academy has successfully trained 500+ students through both offline and online classes. We have conducted 2 chess tournaments along with multiple workshops and summer camps, providing students with practical exposure and competitive experience. Our academy offers training in chess, abacus, handwriting, and creative skills, focusing on building strong fundamentals, logical thinking, concentration, and discipline in students. .

Our Ambition

Our goal is to create a strong learning platform that helps students develop confidence, analytical thinking, and competitive skills, and to expand into a recognized center for excellence in chess and overall skill development..
