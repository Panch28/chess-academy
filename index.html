Who We Are

About Our Academy

Our Background

Established with a strict focus on logical dominance, Sri Sai Chess Academy represents years of pedagogical refinement in competitive chess strategy. We've nurtured over 500+ students to become skilled chess players.

Our Vision

To cultivate the next generation of strategic masterminds and create a global community of critical thinkers through the timeless game of chess.

Our Mission

We strive to empower students of all ages by instilling patience, analytical foresight, and sportsmanship via tailored curriculum and expert mentorship.

Our Achievements

Over 500+ successful tournament victories by our students, multiple state championships won, and FIDE-rated players developed.
